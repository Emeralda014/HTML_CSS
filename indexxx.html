Tribute to my beloved Father

Tribute
Achievement

Words alone cannot describe how deeply hurt I am by your sudden demise. I really wish you stayed long enough to reap the bountiful fruits of your labor. I vehemently know the pain of losing you'll never leave me, but I imagine it will ease with time as I process and get through it but I don't suspect I'll get over it. How can I? How can one possibly get over someone that was there even before you were aware of your existence?

Daddy, you'll forever be in my heart until the very day it stops beating. I am really grateful that I'm becoming a more realized, caring, sincere, passionate, and empathetic person because of you. Still, I would forsake any wisdom for the sake of having you back. I miss you so much.

I love you so much now and forever.

Rest on daddy!
